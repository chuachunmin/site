# Google Site

Google Sites is a free, web-based tool from Google that allows users to easily create websites without needing to know any coding or web design. It’s part of the Google Workspace suite, which means it integrates seamlessly with other tools like Google Drive, Docs, Slides, Forms, and more. With its drag-and-drop interface and customizable templates, Google Sites is ideal for creating personal, academic, or professional websites. One of the most popular uses for Google Sites is building a portfolio website — a digital space where you can showcase your work, skills, and accomplishments.
